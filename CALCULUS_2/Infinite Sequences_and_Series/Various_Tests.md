# Various Tests for Series

## 1. The Integral Test

> Let $a_n = f(n)$ and $f$ is:
>
> 1. continuous
> 2. positive
> 3. decreasing
>
>on $[1,\infty)$, then:
>
>> the series $\Sigma a_n$ is convergent if and only if the improper integral $\int^\infty_1 f(x)$ is convergent.
