# Moment of Inertia

## Principle

> $$
> I = Σ m_i  r_i^2
> $$

## Formulas

1. ROD (the rotate center is the center of the rod)
    
> $$
    > I = \frac{1}{12}ML^2
    > $$
    
2. Solid Cylinder
    
> $$
    > I = \frac{1}{2}MR^2
    > $$
    
3. Cylinderical Shell
    
> $$
    > I = MR^2
    > $$
    
4. Ring
    
> $$
    > I = \frac{1}{2}M(a^2 + b^2)
    > $$
    
5. Solid Sphere
    
> $$
    > I = \frac{2}{5}MR^2
    > $$
    
6. Spherical Shell
    
    > $$
    > I = \frac{2}{3}MR^2
    > $$
