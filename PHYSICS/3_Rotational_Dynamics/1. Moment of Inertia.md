# Moment of Inertia

## Principle

> I = Σ mi * ri^2

## Formulas

1. ROD (the rotate center is the center of the rod)
    > I = M*L^2 / 12

2. Solid Cylinder
    > I = M*R^2 / 2

3. Cylinderical Shell
    > I = M*R^2

4. Solid Sphere
    > I = M*R^2*2/5

5. Spherical Shell
    > I = M*R^2*2/3

### Proof for the Solid Sphere
First for shell: m = 4πR^2*ρ

I = ∫ r^2 *dm = ∫(0,π) (Rsinθ)^2 * ρ * 2π (Rsinθ)Rdθ = mR^2 * 2/3
