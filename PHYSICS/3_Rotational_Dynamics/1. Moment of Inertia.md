# Moment of Inertia

## Principle

> I = Σ mi * ri^2

## Formulas

1. ROD (the rotate center is the center of the rod)
    > $$I = \frac{1}{12}ML^2$$

2. Solid Cylinder
    > $$I = \frac{1}{2}MR^2$$

3. Cylinderical Shell
    > $$I = MR^2$$

4. Solid Sphere
    > $$I = \frac{2}{5}MR^2$$

5. Spherical Shell
    > $$I = \frac{2}{3}MR^2$$
